Publications & talks

Selected publications.

Peer-reviewed research is concentrated in adaptive metaheuristics and applied combinatorial optimisation. Thought-leadership writing focuses on predictive analytics, machine learning in capital markets, and diversity in AI. Plain-language summaries of the three frequency assignment papers are on the research page.
