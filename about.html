About Intelligent Algorithms

Our mission: to turn rigorous research into intelligent systems that organisations can trust.

Our vision

We believe the most valuable machine learning is both research-grade and production-ready — explainable, robust and accountable. We bring discipline of peer-reviewed research to real-world systems and pragmatism of production engineering back to research.

Our journey

We began as an advisory practice in 2014, incorporated in 2018, delivering production analytics for institutional platforms, secured national space-agency research funding for explainable anomaly detection, partnering with leading universities to bring academia and industry together around hard problems in quantitative finance.

Today we work across all areas including capital markets, telecommunications, defence and environmental research — see the domains we cover.
