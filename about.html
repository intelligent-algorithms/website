About us.

Intelligent Algorithms Ltd is a research and engineering consultancy. The practice is small by design: senior, hands-on, and selective about engagement.

The company

Incorporated in 2018, formalising an earlier advisory relationship with a fintech analytics partner that began in 2014. The pre-incorporation period covered prototype development, academic partnership building, and the foundational thought-leadership writing encouraging investment banks to apply predictive analytics to their own transaction data.

Engagements since incorporation have included production analytics delivery for an institutional analytics platform, national space-agency research funding for explainable anomaly detection, and academic partnerships with Oxford, UCL, and Cambridge.

Approach

Work is grounded in the principle that production-grade quantitative systems benefit from research discipline, and that research benefits from production constraints. Engagements typically involve direct hands-on coding, design collaboration with client teams, and clear handover documentation to internal engineering.

Availability

The practice is open to short engagements in two areas: optimisation and metaheuristics consulting (problem formulation, method selection, parameter tuning, code review), and workshop-format teaching for data science teams (heuristics, applied optimisation, data structures and algorithm design, with a focus on practical implementation trade-offs). Enquiries are welcome.
