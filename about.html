About

About us.

Intelligent Algorithms Ltd is a research and engineering consultancy. The practice is small by design: senior, hands-on, and selective about engagement.

The company

Incorporated in 2018, formalising an earlier advisory relationship with a fintech analytics partner that began in 2014. The pre-incorporation period covered prototype development, academic partnership building, and the foundational thought-leadership writing encouraging investment banks to apply predictive analytics to their own transaction data.

Engagements since incorporation have included production analytics delivery for an institutional analytics platform, national space-agency research funding for explainable anomaly detection, and academic partnerships with Oxford, UCL, and Cambridge.

Current technical credentials

Cloud and ML engineering certifications maintained from 2022 onward to stay current with production tooling as it has evolved. All AWS certifications valid to August 2028 unless noted.

  • AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Associate. Cloud architecture, distributed systems, infrastructure design across compute, storage, networking, and security services. Content domains: secure architectures, resilient architectures, high-performing architectures, cost-optimised architectures.
  • AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner. Foundational cloud concepts, AWS service portfolio, pricing and support models.
  • AWS Certified AI Practitioner. AWS AI/ML services, foundation models, responsible AI practice. Content domains: fundamentals of AI and ML, fundamentals of generative AI, applications of foundation models, guidelines for responsible AI, security and compliance for AI solutions.
  • AWS Certified Machine Learning – Specialty. SageMaker, data engineering for ML, model training and tuning, deployment and monitoring, MLOps. Content domains: data engineering (repositories, ingestion, transformation), exploratory data analysis (data preparation, feature engineering, visualisation), modeling (problem framing, model selection, training, hyperparameter optimisation, evaluation), and ML implementation and operations (performance, scalability, security, deployment).
  • AWS Certified Machine Learning Engineer. In progress, 2026.

Approach

Work is grounded in the principle that production-grade quantitative systems benefit from research discipline, and that research benefits from production constraints. Engagements typically involve direct hands-on coding, design collaboration with client teams, and clear handover documentation to internal engineering.

Availability

The practice is open to short engagements in two areas: optimisation and metaheuristics consulting (problem formulation, method selection, parameter tuning, code review), and workshop-format teaching for data science teams (heuristics, applied optimisation, data structures and algorithm design, with a focus on practical implementation trade-offs). Enquiries are welcome.
