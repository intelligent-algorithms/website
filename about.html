The practice

About us.

Intelligent Algorithms Ltd is a research and engineering consultancy. The practice is small by design: senior, hands-on, and selective about engagement.

The company

Incorporated in 2018, formalising an advisory relationship with a fintech analytics partner that began in 2014. Early work spanned prototype development, academic partnerships, and thought-leadership writing on predictive analytics for investment banks.

Since then, engagements have included production analytics for an institutional platform, national space-agency research funding for explainable anomaly detection, and academic partnerships with Oxford, UCL, and Cambridge.

Approach

Production-grade quantitative systems benefit from research discipline, and research benefits from production constraints. Engagements involve hands-on coding, close design collaboration, and clear handover to internal teams.

Availability

Open to short engagements in two areas: optimisation and metaheuristics consulting (formulation, method selection, tuning, code review), and workshop-format teaching for data science teams. Enquiries welcome.
