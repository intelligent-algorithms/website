Applied optimisation & machine learning

Quantitative research and engineering for financial markets and beyond.

Intelligent Algorithms Ltd is a research and engineering consultancy founded by Dr Diane Castelino. The practice combines peer-reviewed optimisation research with production machine learning experience in fintech, environmental monitoring, and applied analytics.

Engagements have spanned client analytics for tier-one investment banking platforms, anomaly detection research with the European Space Agency, sea-ice forecasting with Bayesian deep learning, and sustained academic partnerships with Oxford, UCL, and Cambridge. A second, concurrent strand of the practice is dedicated student supervision: A-level through MSc, with placements at Cambridge, Durham, Exeter, UCL, and King’s.
