How we work

We start with the problem, not the technique.

We start with the problem, not the technique — the process to improve, the decision to make, the question a client cannot yet answer. The domain rarely matters: the mathematics of machine learning, optimisation, and statistical reasoning transfers across fields, and we have applied it in finance, environmental and satellite data, telecommunications, defence, and beyond.

We look for the most effective route, not the most familiar tool, and build to assist rather than replace — clear, interpretable systems, tested against sensible baselines and honest about what they can and cannot do. If you have a hard problem, in any domain, we would be glad to talk it through.
